QUORIDOR

A Game of Labyrinths

How to Play

  • Reach the opposite side of the board to win.
  • On your turn, either Move your Pawn or Place a Wall.
  • Walls cannot completely block a player's path.
  • Hotkeys: Use Spacebar to flip walls on Desktop.
